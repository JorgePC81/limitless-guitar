Eight Limitless Exercises





  • Blues

    This piece shows how to play a blues with, basically, three scales: major, major pentatonic and mixolydian.


  • Rock

    Observe here how I am using melodic phrases by using call-response technique, having a dialog.


  • Funk

    Using the silence as part of the solo



  • The Godfather

    In this a very popular solo, I include techniques like the use of the wammy bar and the quick alternate picking.


  • Song-writing: Before You

    Original pop song. The chords is the first aspect. Melody comes after. See how the chords flow along with the meaning of the song.

  • Song-writing: Momentos

    This original song speaks about the love of a life. The melody and lyrics are writing before, then the music goes accordying to the emotions of the text. This is another method for writing the song.

  • Chord Melodying: All The Things You Are

    This technique shown here is called chord-melodying and it requires control of improv, melody and chords at the same time.

  • Chord Melodying: I Just Called To Say I Love You

    Inside still chord-melodying technique, we can appreciate that it needs a lot of focuss. Furthermore, you will learn the melody then the chords and then the scales needed to play it.
